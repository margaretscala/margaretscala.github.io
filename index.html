Margaret A. Scala

Saratoga Springs, NY

Margaret Scala has been studying multimedia journalism at the University of Maryland for three years now, following a one-year stint at SUNY Buffalo. During her time at the Phillip Merrill College of Journalism, she has mastered the art of interviewing, worked under the wire to complete tasks by deadline, traveled from Baltimore to Washington D.C. to cover stories, and gained hands-on studio experience with Capital News Service. While tackling these tasks in the classroom, in her free time Margaret worked as the Head Manager for the Maryland Women's Basketball team, attending practices daily and working games (and any other times managers were called in) and she watched the team move throguh three Big Ten Tournaments and make it all the way to the Elite Eight in the NCAA March Madness tournament.
